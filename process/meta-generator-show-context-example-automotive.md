# APEX DRIVE 2026 - Show Context

**Project Type:** Luxury Automotive Commercial Campaign
**Runtime:** 60 seconds hero + 3x 15s cutdowns | **Sequences:** 4
**Delivery:** 4K (3840x2160) | 16:9 | February 1, 2026
**Visual Style:** Premium Cinematic Elegance

---

## Visual References

**Primary References:**
1. **Apple "Shot on iPhone" campaigns** - Clean minimal composition, natural lighting, shallow DOF, intimate human moments
2. **Range Rover "Above and Beyond" (2023)** - Golden hour cinematography, sweeping landscapes, vehicle as sculpture
3. **Lexus "Amazing in Motion" series** - Slow motion beauty shots, dramatic lighting reveals, architectural environments

---

## Color

**Palette:**
- **Deep Navy** (#1A2332) - Vehicle exterior, luxury interiors, night scenes
- **Warm Gold** (#C9A961) - Accent lighting, interior ambient glow, sunrise/sunset key light
- **Cool Platinum** (#E8E9EB) - Vehicle highlights, reflections, daytime exterior
- **Rich Cognac** (#8B5A3C) - Leather interiors, warm practical sources
- **Pure White** (#FFFFFF) - Studio environments, clean backgrounds

**Grading:** Warm highlights with cool shadows, lifted blacks (milky not crushed), subtle teal in shadows, golden hour warmth in practicals

---

## Lighting

**Style:** Soft premium lighting - large diffused sources, wrap-around quality, never harsh

**Temperature:** Warm-dominant 3800K with cool accents 5200K, golden hour aesthetic even indoors

**Direction:** Primarily 45-degree key light with strong fill, edge/rim lighting for definition, motivated by windows and practicals

**Atmosphere:** Clean air with subtle atmospheric haze for depth, no heavy fog - premium clarity

---

## Camera

**Focal Length:** 35mm wide establishing shots, 50mm for product, 85mm for detail/human moments

**Depth of Field:** Shallow - f/1.4 to f/2.8 - subject isolation with creamy bokeh

**Movement:** Slow deliberate slider/dolly moves (2-4 inches per second), locked tripod for hero product shots, smooth gimbal for reveals

**Framing:** Rule of thirds premium composition, generous negative space, subject occupancy 30-40% of frame, emphasize environment and scale

---

## Texture

**Aesthetic:** Digital ARRI Alexa / RED Komodo clean, zero grain - pristine commercial quality

**Level:** None - perfectly clean, emphasize sharpness and material fidelity

---

## Characters

**Executive (Lead Character):**
- **Physical:** Well-dressed professional, 40-50s, confident posture, multicultural
- **Wardrobe:** Tailored navy or charcoal suit, minimal accessories, luxury watch visible
- **Lighting:** Soft beauty lighting on face, slightly backlit for separation, warm fill
- **Consistency notes:** Same wardrobe across sequences, natural subtle expressions (no forced smiles)

**Supporting Cast:**
- **Physical:** Diverse ages and backgrounds, upscale casual wardrobe
- **Wardrobe:** Earth tones, blacks, navy - never bright colors
- **Lighting:** Same approach - soft premium quality

**Sequence 1: Morning Ritual - Interior Residence** (15s)
- Visual approach: Soft window light (golden hour simulation), intimate closeups, slow motion details (coffee pouring, watch clasp, leather bag), shallow DOF f/1.4

**Sequence 2: The Reveal - Underground Parking/Studio** (20s)
- Visual approach: Dramatic reveal lighting, vehicle emerges from shadow into light, rotating 360Â° beauty shot, edge lighting defines vehicle lines, cool platinum highlights with warm gold accents

**Sequence 3: The Journey - Exterior Landscapes** (20s)
- Visual approach: Golden hour natural light, sweeping aerial drone shots, vehicle in environment (30% frame occupancy), dynamic tracking shots, shallow DOF on foreground elements

## Prompting Guidelines

**Primary Models:**
- **Image:** FLUX.1 Pro (clean commercial quality), Seedream 4.0 (final 4K hero frames)
- **Video:** Runway Gen-4 (fast iteration character consistency), Veo 3.1 (if dialogue needed), Seedance Pro (cinematic sequences)

**Standard Prompt Prefix:**
```
High-end luxury commercial cinematography, shot on ARRI Alexa, soft diffused premium lighting,
shallow depth of field f/1.8, warm golden hour color palette with cool platinum accents,
lifted blacks with milky contrast, clean digital aesthetic, 35-85mm lens range,
slow deliberate camera movement, Apple/Lexus commercial style
```

**Required Parameters:**
- Image: `--ar 16:9 --s 150 --v 7` (Midjourney) or `ultra` mode (FLUX)
- Video: `--dur 10 --rs 1080p` (Seedance)

**Forbidden Terms:** "gritty", "harsh", "grainy", "dark moody", "dramatic shadows", "neon", "cyberpunk" (maintain premium elegance)

---

## Notes

- Vehicle must always be pristine (no dirt, scratches, wear)
- Environments should feel aspirational but achievable (not fantasy)
- Human moments should feel authentic, not posed or stiff
- Every shot should feel expensive and premium - "you can see the budget"
- Reference rule: "If it doesn't look like it cost $500k to shoot, reframe it"
- Product shots: vehicle occupies 40-60% of frame, never 100%
- Character close-ups: eyes should be in focus, soft bokeh on background
